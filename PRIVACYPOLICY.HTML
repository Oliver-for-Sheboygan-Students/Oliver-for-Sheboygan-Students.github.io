Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03/17/2025

Welcome to Roger Oliver for Sheboygan Area School District School Board! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and safeguard the information you provide when you sign up for our text messaging services.

1. Information We Collect

When you opt-in to receive text messages from Roger Oliver for Sheboygan Area School District School Board, we may collect the following information:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • Any other information you choose to provide through your text messages

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Send you updates, promotions, and important information related to Roger Oliver for Sheboygan Area School District School Board and its offerings.
  • Respond to your inquiries or requests via text message.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We implement reasonable and appropriate measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

4. Opt-Out

You can opt out of receiving text messages from us at any time. To do so, simply reply to any of our text messages with the word “STOP” or follow any additional opt-out instructions provided in the message.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or lease your mobile phone number to third parties. We may share your information with service providers who assist us in operating our text messaging services, but they are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our text messaging practices, please contact us at:
